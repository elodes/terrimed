Welcome!

Clarity Counseling Services is the private practice of Terri Medwed, in Champaign, IL.

Whether you are looking to make a significant change in your life, need some assistance with coping skills, or need someone kind who will listen and be there for you, I think counseling is right for you. At one time or another we all need help to cope with life’s challenges. Other times we feel stuck and have difficulty taking the next important step.

Clients have shared with me that therapy is life altering. Sometimes it can be difficult and not always comfortable, but the outcome of good therapy where there is a trusting relationship between the client and therapist means growth and a positive change in one’s life.

I would be honored to work with you and make a positive contribution in your life. I am so glad that you have visited my website and hope you find the answers to your questions here. If you have any questions please call or e-mail me. I would be happy to discuss any questions your may have about the process. On behalf of Clarity Counseling Services, Welcome!
