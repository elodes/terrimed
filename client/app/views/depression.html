Anxiety & Depression

Are you in a situation where you find yourself worrying too much? Are you sad and unhappy, or usually just feel down?

Do you have to drag yourself out of bed or convince yourself just to get a simple task completed?
Or maybe you just avoid people, family members, or social situations more often than you should?

If you answered yes to any of the above you are most likely dealing with some form of anxiety or depression. Depression and anxiety are serious problems that can darken your vision and distort thinking, while draining the joy and happiness out of your life.

Nearly a quarter of the people in the world will experience significant problems with anxiety at some point in their lives and close to twenty percent deal with some form of depression. Some people suffer from both anxiety and depression.
