Therapy with Children & Adolescents

At CCS I work with children and adolescents starting at age 3 and upward using play therapy for younger children. I have worked with different types of families including divorced, step/blended families, single parent families, gay or lesbian families, military families and families in transition and crisis situations. I begin by working with the family to define the goal of therapy for their child. An individual treatment plan is written for the family member, and we frequently review the plan to ensure that we are making progress. I do not push my ideas or values on families, but work with each family's own unique value system. I lay out various options and make recommendations, but I never forget that it is a parent's choice how to raise their own child.

Children

Play therapy allows children to express and work through their emotions in a safe, warm, and nurturing environment. In addition to sessions with children, I will meet with parents and other family members as needed.

A variety of methods are used when working with children including Puppet Play Therapy, Art Therapy, and Sand Tray Therapy.


Adolescents:

At CCS I work with adolescents on a variety of issues that prevent them from being successful both at home and or school, including:

  • ADHD
  • Adoption
  • Bullying
  • Social Skills
  • Anger Management
  • Oppositional Defiance
  • Eating Disorders
  • Parent or Family Issues
  • Self-Esteem
  • Substance Abuse
  • Depression
  • Cutting/Self-Mutilation
